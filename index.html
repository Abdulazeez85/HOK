Our Story

Born in Ilorin,
Built for the Future

HOK Computers — Home of Khayr — was founded with one mission: to make premium tech accessible to students, businesses, and entrepreneurs right here in Kwara State.

Our founder, Adeyemi Ahmed Abiodun, is a MBBS student at the University of Ilorin who believes technology and healthcare go hand-in-hand. Incorporated with the Corporate Affairs Commission in 2020, HOK Computers has grown into a trusted name in Ilorin's tech scene.
